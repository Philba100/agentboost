   ↓
3. CRM SYNC

   Auto-assign to sales team
   ↓
4. EMAIL ORCHESTRATOR
   Day 0: Initial positioning email
   Day 3: Case study follow-up
   Day 7: Alternative angle
   Day 14: Final attempt
   ↓
5. MEETING SCHEDULER
   If prospect engages: Auto-book discovery call
   Send meeting prep materials
   Create Zoom/Teams link
   ↓
6. NEGOTIATION ENGINE
   As objections arise: Generate counter-offers
   Suggest compromise paths
   Track concession history
   ↓
7. BIDDING ENGINE
   Generate 3 pricing options
   Optimize for margin vs. win probability
   Create formatted proposal
   ↓
8. STRIPE INVOICER
   Create contract + payment link
   Send for e-signature
   Auto-trigger webhook on signing
   ↓
9. CRM SYNC
   Update opportunity to "Closed Won"
   Log all deal data
   ↓
10. ANALYTICS HUB
    Measure: Lead source quality
    Time to close, deal size, margin
    Forecast next month's revenue
